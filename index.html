Our line of work

Cementing

Cemented crowns restore the strength and natural appearance of teeth. A cemented crown looks like the top part of a natural tooth and is meant to provide support while blending in with the rest of your teeth.

Root canal treatment

This treatment is to repair and save a badly damaged or infected tooth instead of removing it, It's intended to prevent it from future microbial invasion

Scaling and Polishing

Scaling is a routine procedure done to remove to remove hardened plaque called calculus which causes teeth damage and gum diseases. After scaling, a polishing paste is applied to improve the appearance of your teeth and remove bacteria

Dentures

These are artificially moulded teeth and gums that are formed and applied to your mouth by your dentists to replace lost or removed natural teeth which brings back your natural smile

Bridges

A bridge is a custom-made replacement tooth or teeth that fill the space where one or more teeth are missing. this permanently restores you bite and bring back the normal look of your jaw

Braces

Dental braces are applied instruments which are used to align or straighten the teeth and guide the teeth to the corrected position sith regard to the person's bite

Minor Dental surgeries

Minor surgical procedures are occasionally performed for removal of badly broken down teeth or impacted teeth that cannot be removed with normal extraction or anything that exceeds caution like the jaw
